On Mon, Sep 30, 2013 at 04:48:03PM +0100, Stephen Eglen wrote:
> Hello,
> 
> I'm just experimenting with i3wm, as my first tiling manager.  Is the
> following possible?
> 
> Within workspace 1, I'd like to have my Emacs window on the left half of
> the window, and keep it 'pinned there' to half the screen.  On the right
> hand half, I'd like the remaining space to be used by whatever
> application (a terminal, Firefox, evince) needs it -- and those other
> windows exist in e.g. tabbed mode.
You can try the following : start emacs, hit split-horizontal if it's not your default, start whatever application you want and focus it, hit split-vertical or split-horizontal, select your preferred mode and finally start some other appications.

I don't know a way of keeping this setup if you close all the application but emacs : since the container for the right half of your workspace wil be destroyed, I don't think it's possible.
