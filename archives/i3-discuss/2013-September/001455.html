On 24 September 2013 22:27, pausiert <pausiert at gmail.com> wrote:
> hello, is there a way to run a script every time a wrong password is
> entered in i3lock? I'm just curious how often people try to get access.

As i3lock just uses PAM to authenticate users, you can use pam_tally2
in combination with faillog (see [1]). Alternatively the unix_chkpwd
(i.e. the regular password checking module) logs failures to syslog,
which you could count.

[1] http://www.cyberciti.biz/tips/rhel-centos-fedora-linux-log-failed-login.html
