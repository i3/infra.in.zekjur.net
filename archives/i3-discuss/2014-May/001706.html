Thanks for this idea. I spent about half a day messing around with
tmux, and I am able to get a status line at the bottom of my terminal.
However I am not going to continue using it, for the following
reasons:

- It makes terminal drawing slow. This is especially bad for e.g.
  navigating though a LaTeX document in vim with syntax highlighting.
- I like the fact that the active window's top bar can be a different
  colour to other windows in i3 :) of course with tmux, all the status
  bars must remain the same color.

I would rather have a top bar natively in i3, than a bottom bar with
tmux. But thanks! I learned many things while playing with tmux :)
