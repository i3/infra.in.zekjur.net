Dear i3 community,

there's a new feature in j4-make-config available. It enables you to 
easily switch between themes for i3. But now it also enables you to 
easily put together a complete i3 config from several different 
configuration parts, depending on the environment you are currently 
working in.

So you can do things like:

- have different layouts and font sizes for different screen sizes
- have one i3bar section for your laptop and two i3bar sections for your 
dual monitor setup at home
- have different keybindings depending on the keyboard you are using
- and much more - any i3 configuration option can be used!

Go check it out at: https://github.com/okraits/j4-make-config

Please feel free to send me feedback and contributions
regarding bugreports, feature requests, improvements,
themes, etc. via mail at okraits at arcor.de. You can also
contact me on irc.twice-irc.de in #i3 and #j4tools.

I'm looking forward to your feedback.

Greetings,
