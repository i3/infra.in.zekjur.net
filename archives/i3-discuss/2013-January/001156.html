Hi Tai-Lin,

Your proposal is an interesting thought experiment, but as pointed out
by others in this thread, I think it neglects some use cases.

Additionally, introducing _yet another_ layout mode is really not
something I am interested in doing. I think i3 already offers a lot of
ways in which it can be used, possibly even too many already.

Since you mention that you find i3’s way of managing windows rather
counter-productive, I’d recommend looking at automatic tiling window
managers such as XMonad, which I hear is very customizable, too, so
maybe that is a better base for your idea.
