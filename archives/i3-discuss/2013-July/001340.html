A quick googling showed that there are different daemons like xxkb and
kbdd doing exactly that. I.e. it has nothing to do with i3.

Further, if you don't want yet-another-daemon, X itself provides a way
to toggle keybindings with one single key (I for example have bound it
to the 'menu' key, as it is completely useless else): the 'grp'
XkbOptions. In my case in the xorg.conf there is:

Option "XkbOptions"
"terminate:ctrl_alt_bksp,grp:menu_toggle,compose:rwin,caps:escape"
