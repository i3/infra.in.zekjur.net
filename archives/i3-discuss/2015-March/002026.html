I just made a script to switch workspaces in i3 in the manner of xmonad.

Normally with i3, each workspace lives on a specific output. When
switching to a workspace, the workspace will show on its assigned
output, regardless of which output has focus.

With xmonad, the output with focus will switch to the requested
workspace. If the requested workspace was already showing on another
output, then the other output and focused output will swap their
workspaces.

Please refer here for more information:
Blog Post: https://travisf.net/i3-wk-switcher/
Github: https://github.com/tmfink/i3-wk-switch

Any feedback would be appreciated!

(Also, I have not tested this on more than two monitors, and would
appreciate it if anyone could help with testing)
