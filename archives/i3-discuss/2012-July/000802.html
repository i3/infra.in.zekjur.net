I've read through the archive and did not find anything regarding my 
problems so here I go recreating my problems:

I open two terminal windows (mod+enter, twice), then move the mouse back 
and forth between them, focus follows.

the other terminal, focus does not follow there.

keyboard to switch the focus it works, but then moving my pointer back 
to the first terminal the focus will not follow again.

right through the dialog into the container behind it.

There are much more issues, if you need more, I will send more.

switch to another workspace and back. But toggling workspaces after
