Hi,

I wanted to share with the group a program I wrote for visualizing the 
i3 tree structure. Here's an example of what it produces:

|$  i3tree
Workspace 1 (horizontal)
         Window foo at bar: ~
         Split (vertical)
                 Window foo at bar: ~
                 Window foo at bar: ~
Workspace 2 (vertical)
         Window foo at bar: ~
         Window foo at bar: ~|


https://github.com/sciurus/i3tree

I hope you find it useful. Pull requests with improvements are welcome.
