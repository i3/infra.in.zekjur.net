Hi,

After upgrading i3 to version 4.2 (from 3.x ...don't recall the
exact version), the colors of the window decorations are unexpectedly
different. For example, this will make a black background:
    client.focused #4D4D4D #000000 #AACCBB

and this will make a white background:
    client.focused #4D4D4D #FFFFFF #AACCBB

But now, with this:
    client.focused #4D4D4D #555555 #AACCBB

...the background looks quite turquoise. I'd expect #555555 to make
for a shade of grey.

Below is the color-related section from my ~/.i3/config file. All the
#rrggbb values are making unexpected colors, biased towards shades of
green. Is there some setting that might be affecting this?

    #                        border      background  text
    client.focused           #4D4D4D    #2C2D2E     #AACCBB
    client.unfocused         #2C2D2E    #2C2D2E     #707678
    client.focused_inactive  #2C2D2E    #2C2D2E     #AACCBB
    client.urgent            #FEA63C    #4D4D4D     #FFE863

    bar {
        colors {
            background #000000
            statusline #ffffff
            focused_workspace  #77AA99 #3D3D3D  #AACCBB
            active_workspace   #77AA99 #3D3D3D  #AACCBB
            inactive_workspace #2C2D2E #2C2D2E  #707678
            urgent_workspace   #FEA63C #4D4D4D  #FFE863
        }
    }
