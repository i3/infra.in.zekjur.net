* John Magolske <listmail at b79.net> [120514 22:58]:
> After upgrading i3 to version 4.2 (from 3.x ...don't recall
> the exact version), the colors of the window decorations are
> unexpectedly different. [...] All the #rrggbb values are making
> unexpected colors, biased towards shades of green.

It turns out this has nothing to do with i3, but rather a setting
in /etc/X11/xorg.conf. When upgrading i3, X was also upgraded and
I regenerated my xorg.conf file. In this section:

    Section "Screen"
            Identifier "Screen0"
            DefaultColorDepth 16

Changing DefaultColorDepth from 16 to 24 fixed the problem, now all
the colors are displayed properly.
