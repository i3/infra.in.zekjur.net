Inspired by Michael's talk recently published by GoogleTechTalks on YouTube,
I'd like to share one observation about reporting bugs to i3wm.

Neither on Contact [1] page, nor in the header of the Trac [2].

IMHO, it's always good to make explicit guideline somewhere.
