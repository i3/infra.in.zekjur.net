> Currently i3 sends event with quite bad timing. For example, the
> workspaces event 'focus' is sent before the tree_render() is called (in
> other words, the focus has not been actually changed, only the internal
> states of i3wm is changed). Although the differences is just fractions of a
> second, it may confuse some applications which rely heavily on the correct
> timing (for example, i3bar with my upcoming patches).
>
> So I add facilities to queue outward events up until the tree_render is
> done.
