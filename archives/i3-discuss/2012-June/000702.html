I am on Arch as well, the problem occurred after the last update (ca 2
weeks ago for me).
Use downgrade (https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Downgrade) to downgrade
xf86-video-intel to 2.18.0-3 and the problem should go away.
I've just checked, and I've downgraded the kernel as well, back to 3.3.2-1,
but I cannot remember if it is necessary or not.

If I remember correctly, then the issue is just in the driver, the
xf86-video-intel package. Downgrade that to said version and the problem
should disappear.
