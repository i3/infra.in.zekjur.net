> moving the newly raised window to another location. If you use a small
> floating window on a regular basis, like I do with a screen ruler, and

This is your mistake. Using floating windows on a regular basis is wrong
when using i3. Floating windows are an after-thought, a necessary evil
for handling popup dialogs in a somewhat acceptable fashion. They are
not an equal alternative to using tiling windows, they were never
intended to and they never will be :-).

I’m not convinced that increasing complexity (mainly mental complexity
for people to understand their choice when reading through the
userguide) by adding the option you propose is worth the benefit.

I’d argue that when you switch to a floating window with your keyboard,
you want to see and use that window, so i3 should raise it.
