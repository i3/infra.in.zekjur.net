Hi,

Good news everyone! I improved our new ticket form.

Have a look at http://bugs-beta.i3wm.org/ (you can play with it all you
want, but please don’t hit "Submit" in step 4 unless you have a real
issue to submit).

It’s a small web-app which asks you a few concise and helpful (for us)
questions. After you answered them all, the ticket will be created in
our trac. The intention is to reduce the amount of round-trips for
tickets caused by questions such as "can you reproduce this with a
recent version of i3?" and "can you please attach a debug
logfile?". Also, the form encourages people to specify their email
address and explains why that’s useful (because we otherwise have to
close the ticket if you don’t respond within 6 weeks).

Any comments and feedback is welcome. If everything works out fine, I’ll
make this the default new ticket form. Please use it when submitting
issues from now on.

BTW: Of course this is not final. I have a few more improvements in the
back of my head once we got this working well, like as-you-type
duplicate search (in tickets, commits, FAQ) or automatic logfile
cleaning/analysis.
