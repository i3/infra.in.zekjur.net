OK, I am not in a good day when it comes to thinking, I guess.

The folder where my personal scripts relies was not in the $PATH, 
because I had modified it into ~/.profile instead of ~/.bash_profile.
So I have added some sleep instructions after every i3-msg commands, 
and can summon a ( very ) basic layout:

#!/bin/sh
delay=0.03s
i3-msg 'split h'
sleep $delay
x-terminal-emulator &
x-terminal-emulator &
sleep $delay
i3-msg 'split v'
x-terminal-emulator &

So, my errors were simple pebcak. Sorry for the noise.
