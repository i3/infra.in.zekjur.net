Julio Molina Soler <jmolinaso at gmail.com> writes:
> laptop needs, I must say, it gets the 80% of everything. But I always
> missed the fact that the volume keys are not working out of the box, and
> turns out it is quite simple to achieve that, so I wonder if it can be
> added to the initial config script, as it can help users to don't bother
> about that small useful feature.
As others have pointed out, it’s not that simple. The following points
are the reason why this will not be added to the default config:

 • different people have different tastes in how the volume should be
   changed
 • different distributions require different ways of changing the volume
 • the keybinding is not generally useful; many machines don’t have it,
   many people won’t ever use it

Thanks for your proposal, though.
