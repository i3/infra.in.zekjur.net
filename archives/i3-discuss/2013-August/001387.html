If you don't know it yet you may find quickswitch-i3 [1] useful. It
creates dmenu menus for workspaces, clients etc.

Regarding your original question: No I don't think this is possible at
the moment. But you could monkey patch it into your dmenu script. For
example, convert all workspace names matching "_[A-Z]" to A-Z. On
return from dmenu you convert A-Z back to the original string by just
prepending an underscore or some similar delimiter. Con: the workspace
name and what you type to get to it are different.

[mabe]

[1] https://github.com/proxypoke/quickswitch-for-i3
